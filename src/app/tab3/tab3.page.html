Offer an incentive to visit marketing strategy Whether it’s a freebie, discount or deal, an attention-grabbing voucher works well for both drawing in new customers and rewarding loyal regulars. our company has add them to existing leaflet designs by adding a perforated edging, featuring our offer. Once we offer is in place, hand them out in the streets to encourage recipients to visit our coffee shop.
